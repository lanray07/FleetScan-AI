# FleetScan AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

FleetScan AI helps drivers, fleet managers, courier businesses, contractors, logistics companies, and field-service teams record vehicle checks, defect notes, photos, maintenance reminders, and PDF inspection reports.

## Data Storage

FleetScan AI is local-first. Vehicle profiles, inspection records, checklist results, uploaded photos, defects, maintenance reminders, PDF report references, subscription state, and business profile settings are stored on the user's device using Apple platform storage.

## Data Collection

FleetScan AI does not require an account and does not collect personal data on a FleetScan AI server in the current release. The app does not sell personal data.

Photos selected or captured in the app are used for inspection records and mock AI defect suggestions on the device by default.

## AI Processing

Mock AI mode is enabled by default. The app includes a placeholder for a secure backend AI endpoint, but no API keys are stored in the iOS app. If production remote AI processing is enabled in a future release, this policy will be updated before vehicle photos, checklist context, driver notes, vehicle type, or inspection details are sent to a backend for processing.

AI findings are suggestions only and must be reviewed by the user. FleetScan AI does not provide legal, MOT, roadworthiness, safety, or mechanical certification.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed through Apple StoreKit. Apple handles payment processing. FleetScan AI may receive subscription entitlement status from Apple so paid features can be unlocked.

## User Choices

Users can delete all local FleetScan AI data from Settings. Users can also remove the app from their device to delete app-local records managed by iOS.

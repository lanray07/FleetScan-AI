# FleetScan AI Terms of Use

Effective date: May 20, 2026

FleetScan AI provides vehicle inspection workflow tools, photo evidence capture, defect reporting, maintenance reminders, AI-assisted suggestions, and PDF report generation.

## Safety Disclaimer

FleetScan AI does not provide legal roadworthiness certificates, MOT certification, mechanical certification, legal advice, or safety certification. Drivers, vehicle operators, fleet managers, and businesses remain responsible for vehicle checks, legal compliance, maintenance decisions, and safe vehicle operation.

AI findings are suggestions only. Unsafe vehicles should not be driven. Critical defects should be checked by qualified professionals immediately.

## Subscriptions

FleetScan AI may offer auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple StoreKit. Subscription management, cancellation, billing, renewal, and refunds are handled by Apple through the user's Apple account.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not rely solely on FleetScan AI for legal compliance, roadworthiness decisions, or mechanical diagnosis. Users are responsible for reviewing and confirming all inspection records and defect findings before taking action.

## Data

Users are responsible for the accuracy of vehicle, inspection, defect, maintenance, and report information they enter into the app.

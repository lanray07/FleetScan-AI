# FleetScan AI Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: `TO_BE_FILLED`

FleetScan AI helps users record vehicle checks, defect notes, photos, maintenance reminders, and PDF inspection reports.

## Local Data

The current app stores vehicle profiles, inspection records, checklist results, uploaded photos, defects, maintenance reminders, PDF report references, subscription state, and business profile settings locally on the device using Apple platform storage.

## AI Processing

Mock AI mode is enabled by default. If a secure backend AI endpoint is configured in a production version, vehicle photos, checklist context, driver notes, vehicle type, and inspection details may be sent to that backend so AI suggestions can be generated.

AI findings are suggestions only and must be reviewed by the user. FleetScan AI does not provide legal, MOT, roadworthiness, safety, or mechanical certification.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed through Apple StoreKit. Apple handles payment processing. The app may receive subscription entitlement status needed to unlock features.

## Data Sharing

FleetScan AI does not sell personal data. If backend AI processing is enabled, data may be processed by your configured backend and AI service providers only to provide app functionality.

## Data Retention

Local records remain on the device until the user deletes them or deletes local app data. Backend retention, if enabled, must be documented here before release.

## User Choices

Users can delete all local FleetScan AI data from Settings. If backend sync or accounts are added, provide account deletion and backend data deletion instructions here.

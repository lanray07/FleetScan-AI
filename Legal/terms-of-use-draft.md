FleetScan AI provides vehicle inspection workflow tools, photo evidence capture, defect reporting, maintenance reminders, AI-assisted suggestions, and PDF report generation.

## Safety Disclaimer

FleetScan AI does not provide:

- Legal roadworthiness certificates
- MOT certification
- Mechanical certification
- Legal advice
- Safety certification

Drivers, vehicle operators, and fleet managers remain responsible for vehicle checks, legal compliance, maintenance decisions, and safe vehicle operation.

AI findings are suggestions only. Unsafe vehicles should not be driven. Critical defects should be checked by qualified professionals immediately.

## Subscriptions

FleetScan AI may offer auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple StoreKit. Subscription management, cancellation, and billing are handled by Apple through the user’s Apple account.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not rely solely on FleetScan AI for legal compliance or mechanical diagnosis. Users are responsible for reviewing and confirming all inspection records and defect findings.
